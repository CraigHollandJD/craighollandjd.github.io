What is AI?

AI is software that learns patterns from information and uses them to assist, predict, or create. Think of it as a tool—not a mind.

What can AI do for me?

AI can help you write, learn, organize ideas, answer questions, and explore possibilities faster than doing it alone.

Does it cost anything?

Is this safe?
